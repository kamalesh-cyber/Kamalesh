Recognised by Leading Bodies

Karunya holds accreditations and approvals from the most respected national and international authorities.

🏅 NAAC A++ — Highest Grade
National Assessment and Accreditation Council — highest grade for outstanding academic practices and institutional quality.

Recognised by the University Grants Commission as a Category 1 Institution for excellence and academic autonomy.
🔩 NBA — National Board of Accreditation
Accredited for quality and high academic standards across Engineering and Management programs.
📋 AICTE Approved
All India Council for Technical Education approval for meeting national standards in technical and professional education.
