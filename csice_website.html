Why Choose CSICE

What makes CSI College of Engineering stand out in the Nilgiris and beyond.

🏔️
Scenic Hill Campus
A 25-acre campus nestled in the beautiful Ketti Valley near Ooty — a serene learning environment unlike any other.
🏛️
Anna University Affiliated
Degrees affiliated to Anna University, Chennai — one of Tamil Nadu's most recognised technical universities.
🚀
Startup TN Knowledge Partner
EDII-TN District Hub for Nilgiris and recognised Innovation Voucher Program partner — supporting student entrepreneurs.
🌍
Industry Placements
Graduates placed in reputed companies including HP, Cognizant, Mphasis, CTS, Multivista Global and more.
⛪
Values-Based Education
Founded by the Church of South India — committed to holistic development, ethics, and community service alongside academic excellence.
Accreditations & Approvals

Recognised & Approved By

CSICE holds approvals and recognitions from key national bodies ensuring academic quality and standards.

📋 AICTE — All India Council for Technical Education
Approved by AICTE, New Delhi for meeting national standards in technical and professional education across all programs.
🏛️ Anna University, Chennai
All B.E., B.Tech, M.E. and M.Tech programs are affiliated to Anna University, Chennai — Tamil Nadu's premier technical university.
🇮🇳 Government of Tamil Nadu
Approved by the Government of Tamil Nadu, ensuring compliance with state-level educational norms and quality benchmarks.
🚀 Startup TN — EDII-TN District Hub
Recognised as the EDII-TN District Hub for the Nilgiris district and Innovation Voucher Program (IVP) knowledge partner under Startup TN.
⛪ Church of South India — Founding Body
Founded and managed by the CSI Coimbatore Diocesan Council, providing a strong ethical and value-based institutional framework.
🏆 Times of India — Best Trendsetters 2024
Recognised by Times of India with the Best Trendsetters Award 2024 for excellence and innovation in engineering education.
