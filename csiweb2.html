Why Choose Us

The CSICE Difference

What makes CSI College of Engineering stand out in the Nilgiris and beyond.

🏔️
Scenic Hill Campus
A 25-acre campus nestled in beautiful Ketti Valley near Ooty — a serene, distraction-free learning environment unlike any other in Tamil Nadu.
🏛️
Anna University Affiliated
Degrees affiliated to Anna University, Chennai — one of Tamil Nadu's most recognised technical universities, ensuring nationally valued credentials.
🚀
Startup TN Knowledge Partner
EDII-TN District Hub for Nilgiris and Innovation Voucher Program partner — actively supporting the next generation of student entrepreneurs.
🌍
Industry Placements
Graduates placed at HP, Cognizant, Mphasis, CTS, Multivista Global and more — with a robust placement cell bridging campus to career.
⛪
Values-Based Education
Founded by the Church of South India — committed to holistic development, ethical leadership, and community service alongside technical excellence.

Campus & Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

A fully equipped 25-acre campus in Ketti Valley, designed for academic excellence and student wellbeing.

📚
Digital Library
15,276+ books, 250 bound periodicals, OPAC online access, journals and internet facility.
💻
Computer Labs
State-of-the-art labs with high-speed servers, Wi-Fi campus coverage, and antivirus infrastructure.
🏠
Hostels
Separate boys and girls hostels accommodating 800+ students with home-like mess facilities.
🏋️
Sports & Gymnasium
Volleyball, cricket, football, kabaddi, throwball, athletic tracks and a well-equipped gymnasium.
🔬
Advanced Laboratories
Discipline-specific labs for hands-on engineering practice across all departments.
🏥
Medical & Cafeteria
On-campus dispensary, emergency vans, and a cafeteria with diverse food options.
🎭
Auditorium
Spacious auditorium for conferences, seminars, cultural events and graduation ceremonies.
🌿
Green Hill Campus
Eco-friendly 25-acre campus in the Nilgiris — one of the most scenic college campuses in South India.
